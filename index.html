About Us

Salve Regina Choir, or SRC for short, was first established in the early 2000s with the help of Rev. Fr. Augustine Tay. With about 30 members, SRC sings for the 6pm Saturday Mass slot every week at the parish

Friday Practices on 1st and 3rd weeks of the month

Primarily a SATB choir, SRC devotes the first and third fridays of the month to meet up and practice. Practice is held in the parish @ Casa Maria from 830pm to 10pm

1 hour before Mass readiness prep

SRC will also run through the Mass hymns and set up the logistics 1 hour before Mass and set aside the remaining half an hour for sacred silence

Additional Practices - Event based

Depending on the event (eg. Christmas Carolling, etc), additional practices may be scheduled to prepare the group for the repertoire
